🖋️ Jaun Elia Poetry

"Shayad mujhe kisi se mohabbat nahi hui."

"Bahut nazdeek aati ja rahi ho, Bichhadne ka irada kar liya hai?"

"Zindagi kis tarah basar hogi, Dil nahi lag raha mohabbat mein."

"Main bhi bahut ajeeb hoon itna ajeeb hoon ke bas, Khud ko tabah kar liya aur malal bhi nahi."
